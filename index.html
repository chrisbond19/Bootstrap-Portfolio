About Me

My name is Chris Hutchinson. I was born on November 19 in Evanston, IL. Since I was a small child, I loved computers and technology. At the age of 5, I took apart my first computer. Although that computer never came back to life, it was a very transformative experience.

By the time I graduated high school, I started working for Apple. I was the youngest worker on my team but I wasn't going to shy away from the moment.
